Want to go on vacation but don't know what to do with your dog?

Doggy Daycare is here for you!

Spas

Each dog will be treated to a wonderful spa where they will relax and play in the water to their heart's content.

First Class Food

All dogs will be given the finest of foods fresh from chiefs who specialize in a dog's diet to give him nutritious, yet healthful food.

Bedding

Dogs will be given the softest and most comfortable beds to sleep in, with an air conditioned room to keep your pups nice and cool while they sleep.

Costs of their stay

There are two price levels that you can pay for your dog's stay here.

Deluxe Plan

In the deluxe plan your dog will be taken on walks every day and they will will be able to enjoy the spa everyday also. Not only that, they will be going to a professional dog groomer to make sure they leave better than when they came.
